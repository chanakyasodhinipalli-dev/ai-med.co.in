CVJ Centre for Synthetic Biology & Biomanufacturing

Kochi, Kerala

Visit Website →

CVJ SYNBIO is a Centre of Excellence dedicated to transformative advancements in synthetic biology and biomanufacturing, housed within the CUSAT Technology Business Incubator at Cochin University of Science and Technology. Founded in 2023 in memory of C.V. Jacob, the centre drives world-class research in personalized immunotherapy, engineered probiotics, lab-grown organoids, microbial bioreactors, and biopharma manufacturing — solving global challenges through sustainable bioengineering solutions.

Synthetic Biology Biomanufacturing Immunotherapy Biopharma

Indian Institute of Petroleum & Energy (IIPE)

Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Visit Website →

IIPE is India’s premier autonomous institute under the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas, established in 2015 and backed by major PSUs including HPCL, IOCL, ONGC, and GAIL. Spanning a 200+ acre campus, it focuses on creating and disseminating knowledge in energy science, petroleum engineering, chemical engineering, and applied computing — with a vision to rank among the top 200 global universities. IIPE brings deep expertise in energy systems, computational modelling, and applied research.

Energy Science Chemical Engineering Computational Modelling Applied Research

GITAM Deemed to be University

Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad & Bengaluru

Visit Website →

Founded in 1980, GITAM is a NAAC A++ accredited multi-campus deemed university offering 100+ programmes across 12 specialized schools. Ranked 88th among Indian universities (NIRF 2025), GITAM has strong capabilities in pharmacy (NIRF Rank 33), life sciences, biotechnology, and engineering. Its Centre of Excellence in Life Sciences & Advanced Biotechnology — developed in collaboration with Stanford Medical School and the Government of Andhra Pradesh — positions it at the forefront of translational biomedical research.

Life Sciences Pharmacy Biotechnology NAAC A++

Andhra University

Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Visit Website →

Established in 1926, Andhra University is one of India’s oldest and most prestigious state universities, holding NAAC A++ accreditation with a CGPA of 3.74 — among the highest in the country. Ranked 23rd in the university category and 41st overall (NIRF 2025), the university has deep strengths in pharmaceutical sciences (NIRF Rank 31), human genetics, biochemistry, marine living resources, and environmental sciences. Its nearly century-long legacy of interdisciplinary research makes it a natural partner for life sciences innovation.

Pharmaceutical Sciences Human Genetics Biochemistry NAAC A++

University of Hyderabad

Hyderabad, Telangana

Visit Website →

The University of Hyderabad is a premier central university and one of India’s designated Institutions of Eminence (IoE), established in 1974. Ranked 18th among Indian universities (NIRF) and 801–850 globally (QS World Rankings), UoH hosts 12 schools and 42 departments across a 1,000+ acre campus. Its School of Life Sciences covers biochemistry, biotechnology & bioinformatics, systems & computational biology, and plant sciences — with 1,200+ SCOPUS publications annually and 49 patents granted. A powerhouse for interdisciplinary research at the intersection of biology, computation, and AI.

Institution of Eminence Computational Biology Bioinformatics Systems Biology
